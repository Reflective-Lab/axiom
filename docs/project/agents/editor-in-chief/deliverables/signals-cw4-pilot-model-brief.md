## Purpose

The article a VP of Operations reads when they're interested but haven't committed. It answers: *What does working with Converge actually look like? What do I risk? What do I get?*

This is the bottom-of-funnel companion to "What Converge Actually Does" (cw2, awareness). Together they form the two-article path from "I'm curious" to "I want to try it."

## Structure

### 1. Why Pilots, Not Demos (200 words)
We do not sell software. We prove it works in your environment, on your data, with your rules. A demo shows what Converge *can* do. A pilot proves what it *does* — for you.

### 2. What a Pilot Looks Like (400 words)
- 4-week, free, time-boxed
- One workflow, one workspace, up to 2 domain packs
- Baseline measurement Week 0, weekly check-ins, before/after report
- Direct support from a Solutions Engineer
- No SLA, no uptime guarantee, no contract until after

Source: `agents/finance-operations/deliverables/pilot-commercial-terms.md` §1

### 3. What We Measure (300 words)
- Cycle time reduction
- Error rate change
- Governance audit completeness
- Agent reliability (convergence success rate)
- HITL intervention frequency

Honest framing: we measure together. If the numbers don't improve, we say so. Publish the "What It Proves — And What It Does Not" pattern from cw2.

Source: `plans/PILOT_METRICS_FRAMEWORK.md`

### 4. What Happens After (200 words)
- If it works: conversion to paid tier (Professional or Enterprise)
- If it doesn't: honest post-mortem, no hard feelings, anonymised learnings with consent
- Pricing preview: link to /pricing, no hard numbers in the article

### 5. Who We're Looking For (200 words)
- Mid-market operations leaders (50–500 employees)
- B2B SaaS, professional services, financial services, logistics
- Multi-step workflows where handoffs break
- Governance matters more than speed

Source: `agents/product-manager/deliverables/PILOT-PROGRAM-PRD.md` §ICP

### 6. Next Step (100 words)
Soft CTA to /pilot application form. Educational tone. "If this sounds like your problem, let's talk."
