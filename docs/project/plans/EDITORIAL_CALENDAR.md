## Content Mix Rotation

The Signals blog follows a four-week rotation to maintain variety:

1. **Technical deep-dive** (Alice) — system architecture, convergence mechanics, trust boundaries
2. **Demo/benchmark/experiment** (Bob) — walkthrough, measurement, tangible artifact
3. **Business/GTM insight** (Blake + Caroline) — pilot model, outcomes, positioning
4. **Industry perspective or guest voice** — external viewpoint, case study, partner story

---

## Editorial Standards (Every Piece)

- [ ] No unverified claims. Source everything.
- [ ] Epistemic labels: **Observed** / **Inferred** / **Speculative** on all non-obvious assertions.
- [ ] Technical accuracy verified by engineering (Eli or Kira).
- [ ] Consistent with positioning: governance, not automation.
- [ ] No hype, no superlatives without data.
- [ ] Funnel stage and GTM tie-in identified before commissioning.
