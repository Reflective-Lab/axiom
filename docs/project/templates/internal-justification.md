### How Converge Works (Non-Technical)

Converge orchestrates multi-step business workflows using AI agents that propose actions, a governance engine that verifies each step is correct, and human-in-the-loop gates for decisions that require human judgment. Every action is provably correct before it executes.

Key difference from other automation tools: Converge is **deterministic** (same inputs always produce the same outputs), **explainable** (every decision can be traced to a specific rule), and **human-authoritative** (humans retain final say on critical decisions).
